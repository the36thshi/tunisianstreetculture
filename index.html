Introduction to the Mediterrean-Shaolin homecountry

Hip-hop, street-wear, graffiti, football... All you need to know about Tunisia's streets. Started in the 80's, the Tunisian street culture is phenomenal and huge in all aspects. With football and the passion it procures, the youth identified in the colors of their teams (red and yellow for "Esperance", red and white for "Club Africain". Being the instigators of the "Ultras" movement in Africa, and one of the first Arab countries to adopt rap, Tunisians have easily one of the most unique street culture in Africa and the Middle East.
